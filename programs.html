Python-Sampler

This terminal program, still on development,
is designed to make sequences of pre-recorded audio
on the minimum amount of UI and resources (using Python).
It lets you manipulate a number of samples with pitch tempo and speed.

Python-Sequencer

This terminal program, still on early development,
is designed to make sequences of MIDI send
on the minimum amount of resources (using Python).
It lets you manipulate a looping sequence sending midi to a
port with note, velocity and time.
